Instructions

Objective of the game:

Try to follow the Queen of Hearts as the cards shuffle face down to win coins!

  1. Make a wager at the bottom of the screen
  2. Observe the starting position of the Queen of Hearts
  3. Click the "Shuffle" button
  4. After shuffle, flip a card to find the Queen of hearts
